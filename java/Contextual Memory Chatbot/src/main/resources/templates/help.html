‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the contextual memory work?
A: The chatbot maintains context throughout your conversation session. It remembers previous messages, questions, and topics, allowing for more natural and coherent discussions.
Q: Is my conversation data stored permanently?
A: No, conversation data is only stored temporarily in memory for the duration of your session. When you close the browser or refresh the page, the conversation history is cleared.
Q: What kind of questions can I ask?
A: You can ask about a wide range of topics including general knowledge, explanations, creative writing, problem-solving, coding help, and more. The AI is designed to be helpful across many domains.
Q: Why isn't the chatbot responding?
A: Check your internet connection and ensure the message was sent successfully. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page to start a new session.
Q: Can I save my conversations?
A: Currently, conversations are not saved automatically. You can manually copy and save important parts of your conversation if needed.
